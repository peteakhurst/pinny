Functions

Main Room

The main room of the pub (also known as 'the wedge') has space for 50 seats with more room for standing, it's also home to our main stage which features acts of all sizes, year round. This is a great area to have a birthday event or a gathering of sizes between 40-60.

Sports Room

The sports room is our newest room, with two tvs and Foxtel. A great place to watch any type of match, or simply your footy team. Also great for small to medium sized groups 15-25 to have a dinner party or just simply your own area to celebrate an occasion

The Beergarden

Our beergarden is our largest space and it features a summer stage, a smoking area, and several long communal tables. This area is fantastic in the warm sunshine, but also good for winter, with our several heaters keeping the area nice and toasty. The beergarden is great for our broadest selection of groups, anywhere from 20-100. We have an easy way to give you your own space while sharing the garden with other visitors.
